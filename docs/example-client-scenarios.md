# Example Client Scenarios

## Scenario 1 — Real estate agency

### Client type

Small real estate agency in Casablanca.

### Problem

The agency receives property inquiries from multiple channels but spends too much time manually qualifying buyers and renters.

### Workflow

AI classifies each inquiry by intent, budget, location preference and urgency.

A human agent reviews the result before responding.

### Tools used

- Website form.
- WhatsApp or email.
- Spreadsheet or CRM.
- LLM.
- Optional n8n workflow.

### Expected output

- Lead category.
- Budget range.
- Preferred location.
- Urgency level.
- Suggested next action.
- Follow-up message draft.

### Business impact

The agency can respond faster and reduce time spent on low-quality inquiries.

## Scenario 2 — Retail or e-commerce business

### Client type

Small retail or e-commerce business.

### Problem

The business needs product descriptions, social content and customer FAQ answers but lacks time to produce them consistently.

### Workflow

AI generates first drafts from product information.

A human reviews and edits before publishing.

### Tools used

- Product catalog.
- LLM.
- CMS or e-commerce platform.
- Spreadsheet.
- Optional automation tool.

### Expected output

- Product descriptions.
- FAQ answers.
- Social post drafts.
- Content briefs.
- Customer support snippets.

### Business impact

The business can produce content faster while keeping human control over quality and accuracy.

## Scenario 3 — Professional services firm

### Client type

Small consulting, accounting, legal or advisory firm.

### Problem

The firm spends too much time drafting proposals, summarizing meetings and preparing client follow-ups.

### Workflow

AI creates structured summaries, proposal outlines and follow-up drafts based on approved notes.

A consultant reviews before sending externally.

### Tools used

- Meeting notes.
- Document templates.
- LLM.
- CRM or spreadsheet.
- Email.

### Expected output

- Meeting summary.
- Proposal outline.
- Follow-up email draft.
- Action item list.
- CRM notes.

### Business impact

The firm can improve documentation quality and reduce repetitive drafting time.

## Scenario 5 — B2B sales team

### Client type

Small B2B company with a sales team.

### Problem

Sales reps spend too much time researching accounts, preparing follow-ups and updating CRM notes.

### Workflow

AI creates account briefs, qualifies leads and drafts follow-up messages.

Sales reps review before using the output.

### Tools used

- CRM.
- LinkedIn.
- Website.
- LLM.
- Email.
- Optional n8n workflow.

### Expected output

- Account brief.
- Lead qualification summary.
- Suggested next-best-action.
- Follow-up email draft.
- CRM note structure.

### Business impact

The team can save research time and improve sales preparation consistency.
