Use a 1-to-5 score for business value, complexity, data readiness and adoption readiness. A high complexity score means the use case is harder to implement.

## Prioritization method

1. Score each candidate from 1 to 5 on business value, complexity, data readiness and adoption readiness.
2. Prefer high-value use cases with low or medium complexity.
3. Check that required data is available, reliable and permitted for use.
4. Confirm that a process owner and human reviewer are available.
5. Select one narrow pilot with measurable success criteria.

## Recommended starting points

1. Sales account research.
2. Lead qualification.
3. FAQ response assistance.
4. Meeting summaries and CRM notes.
5. Product description drafts.

These priorities are directional. Each business should validate assumptions with its own workflow, data and users before scaling.
