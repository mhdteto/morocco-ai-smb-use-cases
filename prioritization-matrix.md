# AI Use Case Prioritization Matrix

| Use case | Sector | Complexity | ROI potential | Suggested tools |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| Lead qualification | Real estate | Medium | High | CRM + LLM |
| WhatsApp FAQ bot | Education | Low | Medium | Chatbot + Sheets |
| Quote generation | Services | Medium | High | Forms + AI + PDF |
| Review analysis | Hospitality | Low | Medium | Scraper + LLM |
| Product description generation | Retail | Low | Medium | LLM + CMS |
| Sales account research | B2B sales | Low | High | LLM + LinkedIn + CRM |
| CRM update automation | B2B sales | Low | High | Meeting notes + CRM |
| Intake form summarization | Professional services | Low | Medium | Forms + LLM |

## Prioritization method

Score each use case from 1 to 5 on:

- Repetition
- Time saved
- Revenue impact
- Implementation complexity
- Risk level

Start with workflows that are frequent, low-risk and easy to review manually.

## Recommended starting points

1. Sales account research
2. Lead qualification
3. Review analysis
4. Meeting summaries
5. Product description generation
