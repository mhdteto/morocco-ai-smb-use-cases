### Purpose

This first version provides a practical library of AI and automation opportunities for Moroccan SMBs.

It is designed for:

- SMB owners.
- Consultants.
- B2B service providers.
- Sales teams.
- Marketing teams.
- Local businesses.
- Founders exploring practical AI adoption.

### Notes

This project does not guarantee ROI, revenue growth or operational improvement.

It provides practical frameworks and examples to help businesses identify, prioritize and evaluate AI use cases.

AI adoption should start with small, measurable pilots rather than broad, vague transformation projects.
