# Hospitality — AI Use Cases

## Overview

Hotels, guesthouses and tourism operators can use AI to summarize feedback, assist with common questions and route service requests. Customer-facing information must remain current and reviewed.

## Common business problems

- Reviews are spread across several platforms.
- Staff repeat answers about check-in, amenities and policies.
- Complaints need fast routing to the correct team.
- Seasonal offer preparation is time-consuming.

## Practical AI use cases

| Use case | Business value | Complexity | Example tools |
|---|---|---|---|
| Review summarization | Medium | Low | Review exports + LLM |
| Guest FAQ assistance | High | Medium | Approved FAQ + help desk |
| Complaint triage | High | Medium | Shared inbox + LLM + routing |
| Offer draft generation | Medium | Low | Content calendar + LLM |
| Local information assistant | Medium | Medium | Curated local guide + chatbot |

## Recommended first pilot

Start with a monthly review summary. Classify recurring themes, service strengths and issues, then ask managers to verify examples and choose actions. This keeps the first use case internal and measurable.

## Example Client Scenario

### Client type

Small riad or guesthouse.

### Problem

Management reads reviews individually but lacks a consistent view of recurring guest feedback.

### Workflow

Reviews from an approved export are grouped by theme and sentiment. AI produces a draft summary with examples for human validation.

### Tools used

- Review platform exports.
- Spreadsheet.
- LLM.
- Reporting document.

### Expected output

- Top positive themes.
- Recurring complaints.
- Issues by service area.
- Suggested questions for management review.

### Business impact

The workflow can reduce manual reporting effort and make recurring issues easier to review. It does not replace operational investigation.

## Risks and limits

- Small review volumes can produce misleading patterns.
- Sentiment analysis may miss language and cultural context.
- Booking policies and local recommendations must be current.
- Urgent or safety-related complaints require immediate human handling.
